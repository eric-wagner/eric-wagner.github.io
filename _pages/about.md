I am a postdoctoral researcher in cybersecurity with the [Systems and Network Security Group](https://www.uni.lu/snt-en/research-groups/sns/) at the [Interdisciplinary Centre for Security, Reliability and Trust (SnT)](https://www.uni.lu/snt-en/) of the [University of Luxembourg](https://www.uni.lu), led by [Prof. Dr. Vincent Lenders](https://lenders.ch/).

Previously, I was an associate researcher in the [Cyber Analysis & Defense](https://www.fkie.fraunhofer.de/en/departments/cad.html) department at [Fraunhofer FKIE](https://www.fkie.fraunhofer.de/en) headed by Prof. Dr. Elmar Padilla from 2020 to 2025. 
I pursued my Ph.D. (Dr. rer. nat.) at the [Chair of Communication and Distributed Systems (COMSYS)](https://www.comsys.rwth-aachen.de/) at at [RWTH Aachen University](https://www.rwth-aachen.de/cms/~a/root/) under the supervision of Prof. Dr.-Ing. Klaus Wehrle from 2021 to 2026.
Before,  I studied computer science at [RWTH Aachen University](https://www.rwth-aachen.de/cms/~a/root/), where I obtained my Bachelor and Master degrees.

My research interests include lightweight cryptography, intrusion detection, automotive cybersecurity, and critical infrastructure protection.
